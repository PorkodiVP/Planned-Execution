WHAT IS PLANNED PILLAR?

  • Planned Pillar represents the centralized book of work of our Client Commitments, Regulatory Updates and Roadmap Items scoped as part of Quarterly Release Planning.

  • This book of work is prioritized by Product Management team and continuously monitored by Project Management team, from inception to delivery, by ensuring that Status, FixVersion and Target Release Date are communicated throughout the process on a regular cadence.
